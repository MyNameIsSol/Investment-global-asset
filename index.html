Frequently Asked Questions

We prioritize transparency and encourage you stay informed about market conditions. Our team is here to support you on your journey.

How do I open my securedglobalasset.com account?

It's quite easy and convenient. Follow this link, fill in the registration form and then click "Register".

How do I make an investment?

To make a investment you must first become a member. Once you are signed up, you can make your first deposit. All deposits must be made through the members area. You can login using the member username and password you receive when signup. Select investment option and choose a plan.

How do i make a deposit?

You can open a free cryptocurrency account by downloading any of the cryptocurrency apps and creating an account with them. Copy the crytocurrency address provided on our membership area and transfer the ammount you wish to deposit.

How long does it take for my deposit to be added to my account?

Your account will be updated as fast, as you deposit.

How do you calculate the interest on my account?

Depending on each plan. Interest on your securedglobalasset.com account is acquired at the end of each plan duration and credited to your available balance.

After I make a withdrawal request, when will the funds be available on my cryptocurrency account?

Funds are usually available within 5 minutes after request is made.

Can I lose money?

It's important to note that, while we have a team of expert analysts who diligently study market trends and conduct thorough analyses to guide our investment decisions, all investments inherently carry a level of risk. Our commitment is to help you make well-informed investment using the most accurate and informed insights provided by our expert. It's very important for you to know that we are real traders and that we invest members' funds on major investments.

Who manages the funds?

These funds are managed by a team of securedglobalasset.com investment experts.
